# Button

Buttons allow to take actions in the app, and are used for navigation, forms, or any other task.
There are 3 different variants `primary`, `secondary` and `tertiary` or the one you decide to create, but also other 4 intents `info`, `success`, `warning` and `error`.

You can customize variants changing the variables, more info in the [variables page](/docs/start--page)

### Vue

```js

  name: "App",
```
